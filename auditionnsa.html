Audition Form for Nepali Night 2019

Nepali Night 2019 will be hosted on Mar 09, Sat from 5:00 pm to 8:00 pm at Bayou Pointe, University of Louisiana Monroe (1 Warhawk Way, Monroe, LA 71203). We will be hosting the first round of auditions from Feb 14 through Feb 17(i.e Thursday to Sunday). Please be aware that you will NOT have to have your entire performance ready by the given date. And if selected to perform we will provide you with another date for second round of audition where you will have to show your complete performance to the judges prior to the actual event. Since Nepali Night is the largest cultural event on campus, we will have a few themes pertaining to our cultures and traditions so please plan accordingly.
